About IEMMUN 2k24

Over the years, the institute has been a keen contributor to the Model United Nations Community. This year we are coming forth with the most awaited 11th edition of IEM-MUN. IEM-MUN 2024 will provide a platform to exhibit knowledge, interpersonal, and leadership skills where the boundaries of every individual are tested, their patience worked, and their intellect challenged. IEM-MUN provides a wonderful platform for young people to solve issues of global consequences and shape their journey in becoming future leaders. Being one of the most eagerly awaited MUN in the city, it is a wonderful opportunity for student delegates to take responsibility, rise to the occasion, and propose solutions to geopolitical issues.

This Year's Committees

UNECOSOC

UN Economic and Social Council works towards sustainable development.

Deliberation on the current state of clean energy access across key sectors with a special emphasis on enhancing the climate resilience of developing countries' infrastructure.

UNDISEC

UN Disarmament and International Security Committee focuses on global security issues.

Deliberation on the proliferation of conventional and unconventional weapons in the Middle East with special emphasis on the ongoing conflict of Israel and Palestine.

UNHCR

UN Refugee Agency provides assistance and protection to refugees worldwide.

  • Promoting State Responsibility in ensuring the protection of displaced people.
  • Addressing the welfare situation of IDPs and asylum-seekers in the Sahel Region and its implication towards the progression of Agenda 2030.

Lok Sabha

The lower house of India's Parliament represents the people of India.

  • Discussion on the formulation of a comprehensive framework for improving the digital literacy rate of the country.
  • Deliberation on the Disaster Management (Amendment) Bill, 2024

IP

Intellectual Property Committee deals with global intellectual property issues.

Photographic coverage and journalistic representation of the conference.
