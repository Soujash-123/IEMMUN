About IEMMUN 2024

Over the years the institute has been a keen contributor to the Model United Nations Community. This year we are coming forth with the most awaited 11th edition of IEM-MUN. IEM-MUN 2024 will provide a platform to exhibit knowledge, interpersonal and leadership skills where the boundaries of every individual are tested, their patience worked and their intellect challenged. IEM-MUN provides a wonderful platform for young people to solve issues of global consequences and shape their journey in becoming future leaders. Being one of the most eagerly awaited MUN in the city, it is a wonderful opportunity for student delegates to take responsibility, rise to the occasion and propose solutions to geopolitical issues.
